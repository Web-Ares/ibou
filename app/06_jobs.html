Graphic Designer

A visionary designer with product experience.
Description
Ibous Agency is seeking an experienced and talented Graphic Designer to work in our Belgium headquarters. The successful candidate will be responsible for art direction and development of creative concepts for the marketing department.
Projects will cover a wide spectrum, ranging from logo design, packaging design and advertising campaigns to company branding, promotion, events, as well as all other marketing materials to support current and future projects.
WE NEED a dude WHO
  • wants to have a huge impact on our products
  • has visionary ideas and experience bringing them to life in real products
  • is eager to discuss and defend their designs
  • wants to work with the team at our awesome office
RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Work with a team of in-house designers and illustrators to develop creative materials from concept to final artwork.
  • Push Ibou's heritage of innovation and unique brand imagery for a variety of video industry.
  • Build strong communication with the marketing team to effectively manage their creative projects.
QUALIFICATIONS
  • Work with a team of in-house designers and illustrators to develop creative materials from concept to final artwork.
